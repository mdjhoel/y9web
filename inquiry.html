iii. analyses a range of existing products that inspire a solution to the problem in detail

iv. develops a detailed design brief, which summarizes the analysis of relevant research.

When writing a design brief
Students should
 Students should NOT
  • present research concisely
  • explain how and why the research is relevant to solving the problem
  • cite all sources of information with links to the original sites
  • write large paragraphs of text
  • include statements that do not relate to solving the problem
  • make statements without evidence from expert opinion or data
  • include all of their research within the body of the project
